Opening up to another possiblity

As humans we use labels on a daily basis, either to slot people into the databases of our minds or even labels we consider ourselves to slot into. Are these labels creating more distance between us, dividing some and brining only some together?

I personally feel labels are fine to use to help us give a general idea about something, the problem occurs when we take these labels too seriously and are not open enough to change whatever the ideas may be that we hold with certain labels. This is when the illusion of distance is magnified and causes far too much conflict because in holding these labels so rigidly we are no longer able to see each other as the same as us.
